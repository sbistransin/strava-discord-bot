Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2025

Introduction

This Strava Discord Bot ("the Bot") connects your Strava account to Discord to share your activity updates with your server. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

What We Collect

When you connect your Strava account, we collect:

  • Your Discord user ID
  • Your Strava authentication credentials
  • Your Strava athlete ID and name
  • Your activity data when you upload to Strava (including activity name, type, distance, duration, pace, elevation, and other activity details)

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Connect your Strava account to Discord
  • Retrieve your activities when you upload to Strava
  • Post your activity updates to the Discord channel

We do NOT:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or analytics
  • Use your data for AI/ML training
  • Sell your data

⚠️ Important: Your Activities Are Shared in a Discord Server

Your Strava activities will be posted to a Discord channel visible to all server members using the same bot.

This includes activity name, type, distance, duration, pace, elevation, and other details from Strava.

By connecting your Strava account, you agree to share this information publicly in the server.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices.

How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your data only as long as your account is connected. When you disconnect, all your data is immediately deleted from our systems.

Note: Messages already posted to Discord will remain visible unless you manually delete them.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Disconnect anytime using the /disconnect-strava command
  • Request data deletion by disconnecting
  • Contact us with questions about your data

When you disconnect, your Strava authorization is revoked and all your data is deleted.

Third-Party Services

Strava: We use the Strava API to access your activities. Strava may monitor our API usage. See Strava's Privacy Policy.

Discord: Your activities are posted to Discord channels. See Discord's Privacy Policy.

Data Breaches

If a security breach occurs, we will notify you and Strava within 24 hours.

Children's Privacy

This Bot is not for users under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updates.

Your Consent

By using /connect-strava, you consent to:

  • Collection and use of your data as described here
  • Public posting of your Strava activities to the Discord channel
  • Processing by Strava per their privacy policy

You can withdraw consent anytime using /disconnect-strava.
